## Working System (2-Person Team)
### Person A: Sourcing & Logistics
- Seleksi peternak & screening kualitas harian.
- Proses cleaning (membersihkan kotoran tanpa air jika memungkinkan).
- Sorting (Grading size) & Packing.
- Pengiriman sesuai rute cluster.

### Person B: Sales, Admin & Finance
- Lead generation (cari perumahan target & hubungi Ibu RT).
- Customer Service (handle WA order).
- Content Management (update IG/Status WA).
- Bookkeeping (pencatatan arus kas).

---

## Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)
1. **Procurement**: Pilih telur dengan kulit paling mulus & tebal.
2. **Quality Control**: Cek berat rata-rata per pack (pastikan "Super Size").
3. **Inventory**: Stok telur maksimal 3-5 hari di gudang (harus selalu Fresh).
4. **Delivery**: Tagihan dikirim via WA sebelum barang sampai (Cash on Delivery atau Transfer).
